Payment Methods:


At Interwood, we aim to ensure that your payment process is as simple and seamless as possible. That is why we offer multiple payment options for your ease:
We accept the following payment methods:
• Debit or Credit Card (Visa and Master powered only)
• Online transfer by bank
• Cash Payment on Delivery (Valid on orders of accessories only up to PKR 25,000)
• Payment in Installments
No matter which method of payment you choose, you can rest assured that our secure payment gateway partners use advanced encryption technology to protect all your transactions at all times. We also guarantee that all of your transactions details are also kept 100% private.

How to Pay Online:


Follow these instructions while paying online at Hudson.pk:
• Log on to www.Hudson.pk
• Browse through our website, select the product(s) that you would like to purchase and add them to your cart
• Click on “Checkout.”
• Create an account by entering your details (one time only) or continue as “Guest”
• Lastly, continue to shipping method and enter your card details for the most secure payment.

Payment security:


Hudson Furnishing utilizes data encryption and online security protocols that prevent fraudulent credit and debit card use. We also regularly perform thorough security checks on all orders received. These checks include issuance of a code against your order and also a follow-up email or phone call for confirmation prior to processing your order.
Our advanced security systems encrypt all electronic transactions and payments in accordance with international data protection standards, ensuring that al l your information remains safe at every step of your transaction. We also ensure that your card information remains protected while it is transmitted to the respective banks for payment processing. To provide an additional layer of security, all online translations are also authenticated by Visa (for Visa Cards), MasterCard Secure Code (for MasterCard & Maestro cards).
